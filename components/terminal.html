Easy Interface
The BCT Terminal interfaces with 80+ exchanges, trading all major crypto-currencies. With secure low-latency access, and a consolidated order book, trades execute at high speeds. All exchanges are accessed by our unified KYC Registration.
Security Hardened
Powered by Linux, our platform boasts reliability, uptime, and hardened security. Only certified devices are allowed to be connected in the closed ecosystem. The platform’s software prioritizes security, efficiency, and simple DevOps community integration.
Token Powered
The BCT Token powers all App subscriptions as an ERC20 token, and is easily stored. Our open App community is offered a revenue share with all approved Apps, encouraging an open development environment.
